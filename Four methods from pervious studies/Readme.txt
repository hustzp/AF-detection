The four methods are from the following studies,
[1] Islam MS, Ammour N, Alajlan N, Aboalsamh H. Rhythm-based heartbeat duration
normalization for atrial fibrillation detection. Comput Biol Med. 2016; 72:160-9.
[2] Hirsch G, Jensen SH, Poulsen ES, Puthusserypady S. Atrial fibrillation detection using heart
rate variability and atrial activity: A hybrid approach. Expert Syst Appl. 2021; 169:114452.
[3] Andersen R.S, Peimankar A, Puthusserypady S. A deep learning approach for real-time
detection of atrial fibrillation. Expert Syst Appl 2019; 465-73.
[4] Tao X, Dang H, Xiong D, Liu R, Liu D, Zhou F. Automated detection of atrial fibrillation
based on DenseNet using ECG signals. Proceedings of the 2020 International Symposium on
Artificial Intelligence in Medical Sciences 2020; 69-73.
